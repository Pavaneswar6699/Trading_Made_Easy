4. REWARD SHAPING & ENVIRONMENT LIMITATIONS

* Sparse Reward Problem: Because rewards are only realized on position exit, the

  in cash.
* Action Masking: The agent is penalized heavily for choosing illegal actions 
  (like selling when flat). This negative reward dominates the loss function, 
  distracting the policy from learning real market signals.
